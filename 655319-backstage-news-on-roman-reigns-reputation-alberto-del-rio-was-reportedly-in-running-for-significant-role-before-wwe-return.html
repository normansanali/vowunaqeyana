Source: The Wrestling Observer Newsletter 

According to reports, even though Roman Reigns has been dubbed the “top guy” in WWE, he’s not being poorly received by anyone backstage.

Normally, his situation might cause some jealousy among other workers, but most people do like him and he’s a nice guy overall, so many people were very happy for his title win a few weeks ago. 

Alberto Del Rio 

Alberto Del Rio was reportedly in consideration for a role in a telenovela with NBC Universal before he returned to WWE. 

A source close to the situation told the Wrestling Observer that Alberto would have made significant money for the role, which would dispute the previous claims that he returned to WWE for a “low money” deal.
