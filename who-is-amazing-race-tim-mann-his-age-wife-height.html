Meet Amazing Race 34’s Tim Mann. The show just kicked off fans are willing to learn more about him as he keeps his personal life private. This article is all about his age, wife, kids, and his height.

Tim Mann On Amazing Race 34

Amazing Race 34 is back on CBS and Tennessee native Tim Mann is one of the contestants. He teamed up with Rex Ryan, former New York Jets, and Buffalo Bills head coach and current ESPN football analyst. They are golf buddies and Rex has also shared about him on his show.

A friend of Tim shared a video of Rex giving a shout-out to him on the countdown. They wrote the caption, “Tim Mann getting another shout-out this morning. Tim, he mentions you at least twice a year on the countdown. I love it, buddy.”

In the video, Rex said that Tim skips work three days for the preparation for Bill’s game. Rex says, “Here is the problem. He ain’t in Buffalo, he is in Nashville.”

Now, their chemistry is put to the test. The two-man team also has a name for themselves “T-Rex (Get It)”.

The 34th season of CBS’s Amazing Race has a brand-new cast of participants. The competitors come from various backgrounds. The former head coach of the New York Jets, long-lost twins, ex-NFL cheerleaders, ballroom dancers, motivational speakers, and Big Brother season 23 celebrities are all visible to viewers. For the first time in the show’s history, the premiere will take place in Munich, Germany, rather than in the United States.

Additionally, the program will visit Jordan’s historic city of Petra, where teams will “perform a Jordanian folk dance and learn the Arabic alphabet, ride a train and witness a scenario reminiscent of the 1963 Oscar-winning film Lawrence of Arabia.”

Host Phil Keoghan teased the presence of a “game-changing element” during the first leg. Austria, Italy, France, Spain, and Iceland are also on the agenda, and the race will end in Nashville.

Joining the two on this season are

Aastha Lal and Nina Duong (eliminated Ep.1), Abby Garrett and Will Freeman, Aubrey Ares and David Hernandez, Derek Xiao and Claire Rehfuss, Emily Bushnell and Molly Sinert, Glenda and Lumumba Roberts, Luis Colon and Michelle Burgos, Linton and Sharik Atkinson, Marcus and Michael Craig, Quinton Peron and Mattie Lynch, and Rich Kuo and Dom Jones.

Tim Mann Wife

Tim Mann is currently married to his wife Margaret “Margie” DeGrace Mann. They reportedly met each other in Bill’s game. They tied the knots before 2010 and currently share a beautiful daughter from their marriage. In September 2010 she shared on her Facebook that she was due on 10 March.

According to Margie’s LinkedIn, she started her career at Meritain Health as Executive Vice President/GM and the regional president in 1992. While she served in the role of executive vice president till 2010, she became served as regional president till July 2022.

Starting from July 2022, Margie has been working at Meritain Health as a head of client management.

On her LinkedIn bio, she writes, “As an executive in Client Management, my mission is to help simplify our clients’ and members’ health care experience. I am focused on being an advocate for healthier living by managing the total cost of care while improving clinical outcomes. Since joining the company in 1992, I have directed several critical areas of health benefits administration and management, and have been involved in multiple successful acquisition planning and integration since the 1990s.”

Margie is a graduate of Sarasota County Technical Institute.

Fun Fact: Margaret is 10 years older than Tim who is currently 51 years old and celebrated her birthday in May 2022.
