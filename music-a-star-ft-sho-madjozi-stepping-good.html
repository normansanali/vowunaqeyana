MUSIC: A-Star ft Sho Madjozi Stepping Good

August 15, 2024
blog

Versatile Ghanaian-UK based musician and songwriter, A-Star returns with another outstanding record captioned; “Stepping Good”.

The talented singer links up with engages trending South African musician and “John Cena” hit-maker, Sho Madjozi to thrill fans and music lovers with this masterpiece.

A-Star gain prominence with the release of his song; “Kupe Dance” in 2019. He’s known for songs like; Don’t Touch Me, Chocobodi, Bogada, Balaya, just to mention a few.

A-Star recently release a three track body of works christened; “Do It For Dad” extended play. The entire EP was dedicated to his late father. According to him, he draws inspiration from Michael Jackson.

In his own works; ‘I never release music for millions of streams and views. That stopped a long time ago. I release music because the spirits told me to make the world dance #KingOfAfroDance.’
