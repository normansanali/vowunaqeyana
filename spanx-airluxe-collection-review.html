Spanx Just Restocked Its Popular AirLuxe Loungewear

September 14, 2024
blog

Spanx lovers and loungewear aficionados, listen up: The brand's popular AirLuxe collection is back in stock, but definitely not for long.

If you haven't heard about Spanx's AirLuxe loungewear yet, the stretchy and soft pullovers and pants have won over tons of shoppers, editors, and even Oprah since its launch last October thanks to its pillow-soft feel. When Spanx gifted Oprah the collection during the holidays, she said that the lounge set "feels like a hug," and even deemed the styles as her "look" and "uniform," according to Oprah Daily.

Spanx AirLuxe Collection

Made with a silky, lightweight fabric, the AirLuxe set features a high-neck cropped pullover, a tapered jogger, wide-legged pants, and a longer pullover designed to cover your front and back. The pieces have a drapey but elevated look, so you can look put together while staying super comfortable. An InStyle ecommerce writer who reviewed the collection said the sleek design of Spanx's At the Hip Pullover and Tapered Pant "look flattering enough to wear outside of the house."

Every piece in the AirLuxe collection is sold separately and available in black or navy from sizes XS to 3XL. Spanx told InStyle that the AirLuxe collection has sold out multiple times, so we'd add your favorite styles to your cart before they're gone again.
