How did John Mahoney start his Professional Career?

Going forward, he started his career by establishing a name for himself in the Chicago theatrical community before going on to appear in plays. As a member of the renowned Steppenwolf Theatre Company, he worked alongside well-known actors such as Laurie Metcalf, Gary Sinise, and John Malkovich.
