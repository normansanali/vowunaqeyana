Multiple problems template Jeff T. Thomas (also known as Jeff Thomas) is a music video and television director from the United Kingdom. Travis, Embrace, Zero 7, Ash, Funeral for a Friend, Paulo Nutini, and Jet are among the musicians for whom he has directed over 100 music videos. Commercials for Audi, Nissan, PlayStation, Amnesty International, T-Mobile, Renault, Vauxhall, and Vodafone were directed by Thomas. Thomas directed and produced the pilot and second episode of The Assets, an espionage thriller set during the Cold War, as well as episodes of Wayward Pines, Person of Interest, Vegas, Almost Human, and Fringe. He has also directed television series including CSI:NY and Without a Trace. He was born in South Wales and now lives in Hollywood Hills, California.
