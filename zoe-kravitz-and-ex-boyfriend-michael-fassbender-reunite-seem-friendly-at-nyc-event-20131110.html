Zoe Kravitz and Ex Michael Fassbender Reunite, Seem Friendly at Event

August 08, 2024
blog

First class exes! Former couple Zoe Kravitz and Michael Fassbender reunited in New York City on Wednesday, Oct. 9 at a screening of the actor's newest film, The Counselor. The X-Men: First Class costars caught up with each other during a cocktail party at the event.

An eyewitness tells Us Weekly that Fassbender, 36, spent much of his time before the screening, hosted by Cinema Society & Emporio Armani, chatting with Kravitz, 24. "The seemed like very good friends," the insider tells Us. But as soon as the screening of his film got underway, Fassbender went his separate way.

Despite their 12-year age difference, the two started dating while filming X-Men in 2010. At the time a source told us, "They're really low-key, but they have a great time together just talking music or movies."

The couple split in 2011, however, and Fassbender went on to date his Shame costar Nicole Beharie. The actor confirmed they were dating in the June 2012 issue of GQ, and, seven months later, Beharie revealed they broke up on Twitter in January 2013.
