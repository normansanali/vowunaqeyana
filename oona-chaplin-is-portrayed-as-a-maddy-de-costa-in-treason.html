Oona Chaplin Is Portrayed As A Maddy de Costa In Treason

September 30, 2024
blog

The internationally regarded Spanish-Swiss actress Oona Chaplin plays the important role Maddy de Costa in Netflix’s newest immensely compelling spy-thriller drama, Treason. On December 26, 2022, the series was published exclusively on Netflix. Matt Charman is the creator of the thrilling limited series. He also collaborated with Amanda Duke to write the series. Treason was directed by Louise Hooper and Sarah O’Gorman.

According to the Netflix miniseries’ official synopsis:

“When the past comes back to haunt the newly appointed chief of MI6, in the guise of a Russian agent with whom he has a convoluted history, he is forced to rethink everything and everyone in his life. Secrets, falsehoods, and diplomatic ties will be exposed.”

The series has received a lot of praise for its captivating plot, photography, and performance by the cast members, particularly Oona Chaplin as Maddy de Costa. Without further ado, let’s dive right in and learn all we can about the actress as Treason premieres on Netflix.

Learn about actress Oona Chaplin, who plays Maddy de Costa in Treason.

Oona Chaplin, 36, is the daughter of famed actress Geraldine Chaplin and acclaimed cameraman Patricio Castilla. She is the granddaughter of Charlie Chaplin, the famed actor, and director. She is also the great-granddaughter of the famed Irish-American writer Eugene O’Neill. The Spanish-Swiss actress is most recognized for her roles as Jess in the 2013 film Powder Room, Ruth Levinson in the 2015 film The Longest Ride, Jeanette in the popular TV series Sherlock, Talisa Maegyr in Game of Thrones, and Zilpha Geary in the 2017 TV series Taboo.

Oona Chaplin has also appeared in Quantum of Solace, Pelican Blood, The Devil’s Double, Imago Mortis, What if…, Realive, Aloft, Dancing for My Havana, My Dinner with Hervé, Anchor and Hope, Purgatorio, Inconceivable, and a few more films and television shows. Other notable TV shows in which she has appeared include Married Single Other, The Hour, Inside No. 9, The Comey Rule, The Crimson Field, Dates, Black Mirror, Made for Love, Spooks: Code 9, and a few more.

Oona Chaplin discussed Treason and her character Maddy de Costa in a recent interview with Hello Magazine. She stated:

“Treason is a traditional espionage program in some aspects, but it’s also a family drama in others. And I guess what I enjoy about it is that it delves into what it’s like to be a spy in the upper offices of MI6. As a result, the kids, Ella and Callum, play an important role. Then there’s Maddy, Adam’s wife, and she’s one of the most lovely and compassionate characters I’ve ever portrayed.”

The actress went on to say:

“Her capacity for love is enormous. As an example, she has adopted Charlie’s children. They’re adult children, but the way she loves and cares for them and is really, truly there for them in really deep and significant ways motivated me to learn from that character.”
