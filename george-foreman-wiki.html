George Foreman Wiki:- On January 10, 1949, George Edward Foreman, an American author, businessman, priest, and former professional boxer, was born. Between 1967 and 1997, he competed in boxing under the name “Big George.” Foreman has two heavyweight world titles and an Olympic gold to his name. He is well known for creating the George Foreman Grill.

George Foreman Wiki

After having a difficult upbringing, Foreman decided to try amateur boxing, and at the 1968 Summer Olympics he won gold in the heavyweight division. George turned professional the next year, and in 1973, he upset Joe Frazier in the second round to win the heavyweight championship. George twice successfully defended the title until losing to Muhammad Ali in renowned Rumble 1974, which marked his first defeat in the professional ring.
