Dua Lipa Puts the Exposed Bra Trend into Practice

September 03, 2024
blog

Dua Lipa is the latest celebrity to champion a timeless street style trend: the exposed bra.

Today, the Grammy-winning singer shared photos of her latest outfit with fans on Instagram. She wore the sultry ensemble during a trip to Morocco, where she was busy filming a new campaign for YSL Beauty.

The head-to-toe Saint Laurent outfit consisted of a crisp white collared top, which she wore semi-unbuttoned, and a black triangular bralette underneath. She paired the classic black-and-white look with black leather trousers and a black belt with a gold buckle. Accessories included sculptural gold hoop earrings and a gold chain necklace. Her makeup coordinated with her golden jewelry: shimmering gold-flecked eye shadow, plus a light brown lip.

In a video included in the post, Lipa swapped her white button-down for a slouchy ribbed T-shirt, also in white. Later, she seemingly took a break from filming by changing into a black T-shirt, a bubblegum pink bathing suit, and low-rise blue jeans.

She previously talked about her personal style during a 2018 interview with Refinery29.

"I've never really wanted to put myself in a box and say, This is my style and these are my style staples. I love to experiment and play around," she said. "It's kind of like music: The sound doesn't just include the face of the artist. And the artist should wear the clothes, not the other way around. Fashion can't be forced."
