How Fergie Amassed Her $45 Million Net Worth Since Divorcing Josh Duhamel

Fergie, whose real name is Stacy Ann Ferguson, is a talented artist who has had a phenomenal career in the music business. She first became known as a member of the Black Eyed Peas and quickly became a household name. Fergie then branched out with a successful solo career.

Fergie's net worth of $45 million reached after getting divorced from actor Josh Duhamel shows that, despite problems in her personal life, she has stayed focused on her job and continues to work hard to be a big name in the entertainment industry. Below, you can find out a little more about what she has been up to recently and exactly how she grew her bank account as we break down her net worth.

9 Fergie's Early Life And Career Beginnings

Fergie was born in Hacienda Heights, California, on March 27, 1975. She loved to sing and dance from a young age, so she started performing at local talent shows. It wasn't long before she was doing commercials, and in 1989 she got a part alongside Jennifer Love Hewitt on Disney's Kids Incorporated.

Then, in 1992, Fergie joined the all-girl band Wild Orchid. After signing a deal with RCA Records, they put out two albums. Unfortunately, they were never very popular. Fergie refused to give up, though. She kept moving forward to what eventually would make her a world-wide superstar.

8 Fergie's Net Worth Really Grew After Her Breakthrough With The Black Eyed Peas

Fergie's career reached new heights when she joined the Black Eyed Peas in 2003. The group, consisting of Will.i.am, Apl.de.ap, and Taboo, underwent a serious transformation by replacing their original singer Kim Hill with the "Fergalicious" singer. Their music suddenly became a fusion of hip-hop, pop, and R&B, which resonated with a huge audience.

7 Her Solo Music Career Increased Fergie's Net Worth

While still a member of the Black Eyed Peas, Fergie decided to also pursue a solo music career. In 2006, she released her debut solo album, The Dutchess. The album was a massive commercial success, with hit singles such as "London Bridge," "Glamorous," and "Big Girls Don't Cry."

The Dutchess earned critical acclaim and cemented Fergie's status as a solo artist. It went on to sell millions of copies worldwide and became one of the best-selling albums of the year. The album's success significantly contributed to Fergie's net worth.

6 Fergie's Endorsement Deals

Fergie's marketability extended beyond music. She became a fashion icon known for her often revealing, over-the-top, glamorous outfits. Being photographed in everything from daily wear to red carpet attire earned her a partnership with the brand MAC Cosmetics.

5 Fergie's Acting Career

In addition to her music and endorsement deals, Fergie pursued acting opportunities. She appeared in films such as "Poseidon" (2006) and "Nine" (2009). While her acting career didn't reach the same level of success as her music career, it contributed to Fergie's net worth and overall income.

4 Fergie's Return To The Black Eyed Peas

After a hiatus to focus on her solo career, Fergie reunited with the Black Eyed Peas. They released their album The E.N.D. in 2009, featuring hits like "Boom Boom Pow" and "I Gotta Feeling." The album was hugely successful and pointed the group right back at the top charts again.

3 Fergie's Net Worth Got A Boost With Her Successful Entrepreneurship

Fergie's desire to be a successful businesswoman led her to try out different business ideas. In 2009, she started her shoe line, "Fergie Footwear," with Brown Shoe Company. She told Fashion Magazine that her shoe line is like a metaphor for her. "Walk your path with pride; walk it with confidence." Fergie is the perfect example of someone who walks with confidence, so this is definitely true for her.

Fergie also got into the wine business when she became a co-owner of the winery Ferguson Crest with her father in California's Santa Ynez Valley in 2013. She was very involved in it with her family and had a lot of unique names for the wine. This was her father's dream, and it did very well in the sales department. Sadly, Fergie decided to part ways with the winery after the death of her father in 2021. According to Architecture Digest, it sold for around $3.7 million.

2 Fergie's Personal Life And Divorce

Fergie's personal life has been the subject of public interest. She married actor Josh Duhamel in 2009, and the couple welcomed their son, Axl Jack Duhamel, in 2013. Their marriage seemed idyllic from the outside, but in 2017, Fergie and Duhamel announced their separation. Their divorce was finalized in 2019.

While divorce can be emotionally challenging, Fergie continued to focus on her career and personal growth during this period, and her commitment to motherhood is first and foremost the highlight of her life. She has proven time and again that she is resilient and determined to succeed.

1 Fergie's Recent Projects

In 2017, when she and Josh Duhamel split, Fergie released the album Double Dutchess. She had been working on it for four years, but obviously having a baby during that time put things on pause for her.

She was the host of The Four: Battle for Stardom, a reality competition show on Fox that ran for two seasons and had other celebrities like Sean "Diddy" Combs, DJ Khaled, and Meghan Trainor on the panel. Fergie has also been collaborating with other artists. In 2020, she joined forces with Nicki Minaj for the song "You Already Know."

One thing is for sure: Fergie is not done yet. She is full of talent and hasn't slowed down much since the beginning of her career. We are sure to see more out of her soon.
