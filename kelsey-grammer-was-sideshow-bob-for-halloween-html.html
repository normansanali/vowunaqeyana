Kelsey Grammer As Sideshow Bob Is the Best Halloween Costume

August 19, 2024

2016 has for the most part felt like a dank, urine-soaked hellhole, but one look at Kelsey Grammer’s Sideshow Bob Halloween costume and you’ll be magically transported to some kind of glorious, pee-pee-soaked heckhole. Grammer’s spouse, Katye Walsh, appears to be dressed as Bob’s beautiful, half-bovine, multiple-nippled, humanoid wife or something. (Must be from a recent season.) This casual version of Bob seems like he could have easily accessorized with a rake, but still: wonderful.
