Hunter Harvey is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Baltimore Orioles of Major League Baseball (MLB).

Hunter Luke Harvey was born on December 9, 1994, in Catawba, North Carolina, United States. He is the son of former Major League Baseball pitcher Bryan Harvey and was raised alongside his brother Kris Harvey, who played in Minor League Baseball from 2005 to 2012.

Hunter Harvey attended Bandys High School in Catawba, North Carolina. He was selected by the Baltimore Orioles in the first round of the 2013 Major League Baseball draft. He signed with the Orioles on June 20, 2013.

Hunter Harvey made his professional debut that season for the Gulf Coast Orioles of the Rookie-level Gulf Coast League. He was promoted to the major leagues on August 17, 2019, and made his major league debut that night against the Boston Red Sox.
