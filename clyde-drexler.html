Biography

Clyde Drexler was born in New Orleans, LA on June 22, 1962. Hall of Fame guard/forward known as “Clyde the Glide,” who won an Olympic gold medal with the legendary Dream Team in 1992. He was selected to 10 NBA All-Star games as a member of the Portland Trail Blazers and Houston Rockets and won an NBA Championship with the Rockets in 1995. He played alongside Charles Barkley on the Dream Team and on the Rockets from 1996 to 1998. He was part of ‘Phi Slama Jama’ with Hakeem Olajuwon on one of the best college basketball teams in history at the University of Houston.
