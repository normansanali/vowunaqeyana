LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: The limited series ‘Transatlantic’ is presently streaming on Netflix. It is a period drama based on the true story of Varian Fry, an American journalist, who with his supporters and the Emergency Rescue Committee (ERC), assisted thousands of refugees to flee Nazi-occupied France. The historic drama sets the theme of World War II. It is based on the book 'The Flight Portfolio' by Julie Orringer.

The series includes Gillian Jacobs, known from ‘Community’ and ‘Gotham’ alum Cory Michael Smith as the lead. The limited series on Netflix is co-created by Anna Winger and Daniel Hendler. The show is directed by Stephanie Chuat, Veronique Reymond, and Mia Meyer. Winger is also producing 'Transatlantic' alongside Camille McCurry.
