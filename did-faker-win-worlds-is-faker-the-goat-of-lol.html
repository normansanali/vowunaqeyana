Did Faker win Worlds? Is Faker the goat of LoL?

July 23, 2024
blog

Lee Sango-hyeok known as Faker is a South Korean professional League of Legends player for T1. He is widely considered to be the best league of legends player of all time.

Lee is one of the only two players to have won the League of Legends Championship three times, having done so in 2013, 2015 and 2016. He has also won the All-Star Paris 2012, the Mid-Season Invitational tournament in 2016 and 2017, and the IEM World Championship in 2016.

Did Faker win Worlds?

Faker has won 3 World Championships (2013, 2015 and 2016) and 2 mid-season invitationals, and has 10 combined LCK and Champions Korea titles. He is sometimes referred to as The Unkillable Demon King by Korean fans.

Is Faker the goat of LoL?

One of the reasons Faker is considered goat is the number of championships he has taken. Throughout his career, he has won nine LCK championships in 11 final appearances, the most among all players.

By picking Vex and Tryndamere this season, Faker has played 70 champions on record in the LCK.
