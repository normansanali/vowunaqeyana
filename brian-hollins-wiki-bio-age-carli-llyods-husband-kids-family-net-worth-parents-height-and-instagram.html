Brian Hollins Biography – Brian Hollins Wiki

Brian Hollins is an American former professional golfer and the husband of American professional soccer player, Carli Llyod.

Hollins attended the Golf Academy of the South in Altamonte Spring, Florida. He served an assistant golf pro at the Trenton Country Club in New Jersey. Prior to that he worked as a teaching pro and assistant pro at Will Brook Country Club and also spent three years working at the Laurel Creek Country Club.

In addition working on the country club circuit, Hollins has tried to advance his own career and spent time trying to qualify for the PGA Tour and also competed on the Tarheel and Hooters Tours.
