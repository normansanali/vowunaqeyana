American football quarterback Zach Wilson plays for the NFL team the New York Jets. He is the former college football player representing BYU and one of the most noticeable athletes in the 2021 NFL Draft. 

Zach Wilson Bio & Family: Wiki, Bio, Age

The player was born Zachary Kapono Wilson on 3 August 1999 in Draper, Utah. His parents are Michael and Lisa Wilson (nee Neeleman). His other family comprises three brothers Isaac, Josh, and Micah Wilson, all into college playing careers, and two sisters: Whitney and Sophie Wilson. 


Zach is part Hawaiian on his dad’s side, with his middle name Kapono that means righteous in the same language. He went to Corner Canyon High School and later chose Brigham Young University per wiki. The athlete is 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 m) tall in height and weighs around 97 kg.

Personal Life: Is Zach Wilson Dating?

Yes, the 22-year-old is already off the market. His girlfriend’s name is Abbey Gile to whom he has been dating since their high school days in 2017. His girlfriend believes him to be the most selfless, kind, and hardworking person and considers her the luckiest girl in the world when around him. 


She is super proud of Zach and everything he has accomplished and believes the best is yet to come. His girlfriend celebrates her birthday on 2 June per wiki. On the other hand, Zach believes his girlfriend to be his biggest fan. 

Career Facts: NFL & Contract

Before his inclining NFL career, Zach had an incredible run while playing for BYU. In 2018, he finished the season with 120 of 182 passes for 1578 yards including three interceptions and 12 touchdowns. The next season, he was the recipient of BYU’s MVP and 202 Polynesian College Football Player of the Year Award. 

In the 2020 season in 12 games, he made 247 of 336 passes for 3692 yards, three interceptions, and 33 touchdowns. The next year, he got the fortune to be part of the 2021 NFL Draft, where the Jets made him a part of their team in the second overall selection. On 29 July 2021, he and the team signed a four-year contract worth $35.15 million also a $22.9 million signing bonus with a fifth-year option. 

The young gun made his professional debut on 14 August 2021 during the Jets‘ first preseason game against the New York Giants, completing six of nine passes for 63 yards.
