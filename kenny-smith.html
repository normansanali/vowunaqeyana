Kenny Smith

Kenny Smith, also known as “the Jet”, is a former professional basketball player and a current sports commentator.

He played in the NBA for 10 years, winning two championships with the Houston Rockets.

He also represented the United States in the 1986 FIBA World Championship, winning a gold medal.

After retiring from playing, he became a basketball analyst for Inside the NBA on TNT, where he has won several Emmy awards for his insightful and humorous commentary.

He is also an author of a book called Talk of Champions, which shares his insights on leadership, teamwork, and success.

Kenny Smith was born in New York City and played college basketball at North Carolina under legendary coach Dean Smith.
