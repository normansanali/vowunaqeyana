What Has Bad Girls Club Star Natalie Nunn Been Up To Since The Show Ended?

September 02, 2024

Natalie Nunn is the controversial reality star who made a name for herself in the fourth season of Bad Girl Club between 2009 and 2010. After the show had ended, the TV personality had become so popular that the producers of the BGC decided to bring her back for several appearances to help boost ratings for the follow-up seasons, which appeared to be a smart move because Natalie certainly knew how to draw in a lot of viewers.

Aside from Bad Girls Club, however, the 35-year-old has remained in the headlines throughout the years thanks to her stint on the UK version of Celebrity Big Brother in 2018, where she was reportedly paid $130,000 for her involvement, having also gotten married, welcomed a child, and launched her own makeup line.

So, to say that Natalie has been working hard would be an understatement to all that she’s accomplished in the last few years. She might even be working harder than Kris Jenner -- if that's even possible -- but what else has she been up to?

What Has Natalie Nunn Been Up To?

Since 2010, Natalie has appeared on endless TV shows, including Bad Girls Club All-Star Battle, Love Games: Bad Girls Need Love Too, Hair Battle Spectacular, Bridezillas, and Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars, just to name a few.

In 2018, she signed on for Celebrity Big Brother in the UK, with producers spending a reported $130,000 just to get Natalie to sign on the dotted line and participate in the show, which included former actress Kirstie Alley (who was paid $240,000), and model Chloe Ayling.

The latter found herself in a widespread media scandal with the British press after claiming that Natalie had slept with Dan Osbourne - her former Big Brother housemate - who is married to soap star Jacqueline Jossa, insinuating that Natalie and Dan both cheated on their spouses after leaving the house.

The mother of one later confessed to Chloe’s claims, admitting that not only did she sleep with Dan, they even shared a threesome with Chloe, telling The Sun: “I had sex with Dan in my hotel room. Chloe’s story was true.  I was drunk and it was a huge mistake but it happened.

“As soon as Chloe’s story broke in The Sun Dan began messaging me. He was saying, ‘deny it, deny it, deny it,’ but I told him I couldn’t lie. I chose to say nothing until now. I want to speak the truth. Dan knows what happened that night and he is lying about it. We did have had sex and he got together with Chloe.”

Dan, who remained in denial about both Chloe and Natalie’s claims, later came forward and said: “I want to publicly say I know I have made mistakes. I’ve made some that have almost cost me my family. I have seriously learned from these massively, so I want to publicly apologize to my beautiful wife and my family for the mistakes I’ve made.”

In 2016, the California native released her own fitness workout DVD, titled No Payne No Gain, as she helped thousands of fans start a fitness plan that accommodated health needs, consequently giving them the body results they’ve been yearning for.

In 2017, Natalie launched her makeup brand, Natalie Nunn Cosmetics, where she sells everything from lip kits to eyelashes, beauty palettes, lip gloss, and so much more. When she launched her latest eyelash collection in October 2020, she flew her team out to the Bahamas on a private jet to celebrate the occasion, so there’s little to no doubt that this bad girl is earning some serious bucks through her cosmetics brand.

In 2017, Natalie gave birth to her firstborn, Journey Ruth Payne, just three years after announcing her first pregnancy only to find out she suffered a miscarriage in February 2015.
