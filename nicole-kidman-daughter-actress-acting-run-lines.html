Nicole Kidman's Daughter Wants to Be an Actress, Mom Helps Her Run Lines

July 13, 2024
blog

Nicole Kidman has been playing acting mentor to someone very close to her recently: her 8-year-old daughter, Sunday Rose.

On a red carpet interview with E! News, when asked if she had a Golden Globes dress picked out for the awards show on Sunday, she said all of that has already been sorted, so as to make time for some more important things like her family, and getting the kids ready to head back to school.

"I gotta have my things organized, my daughter just got cast in her school play, so, that's been the main priority, learning lines with her," she revealed. "I was just on the phone with my husband [Keith Urban] and Sunday was in the background going, 'You've got to get off the phone, and help me with my lines, dad,'" she said laughing. "He ran lines with me and now he’s running lines with his daughter." How's that for a full circle!

Kidman and Urban have two daughters together, 8-year-old Sunday Rose, and 6-year-old Faith Margaret.

The 49-year-old actress is nominated for a Golden Globe for Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role in a Motion Picture, for her performance in the film Lion. We're excited to see the dress that Kidman has all picked out during Sunday's broadcast of the Golden Globe Awards, at 8 p.m. ET on NBC.
