What happens if you freeze tapioca pudding?

Unlike many other puddings, its texture will change only slightly and it will not lose its flavor when frozen. Tapioca pudding will last up to three months in the freezer and is easy to thaw. Tapioca puddings are one of the best desserts to freeze, and the best part is, you won’t lose any of its sweet creaminess.
