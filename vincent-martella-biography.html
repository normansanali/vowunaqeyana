Who is Vincent Martella?

Vincent Martella is a singer, voice actor, and actor belonging to America.

Vincent Martella is well-recognized for his role on the UPN/CW sitcom Everybody Hates Chris as Greg Wuliger.

Besides, he is also popular for lending his voice in the Disney XD original animated show Phineas and Ferb for Phineas Flynn’s character.

Vincent Martella: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Vincent Martella was born to his parents Donna (mother) and Michael Martella on 15th October 1992 in Rochester, New York. His father runs Captain Tony’s, a chain of pizzerias.

He holds Italian ethnicity and belongs to American nationality. He migrated to Florida during his childhood and spent most of his time there.

Education History

In discussing his educational background, Vincent Martella attended DeLand High School and got graduated there in 2011.

Currently, he has been studying at the University of Florida for an online degree in business.

Vincent Martella: Profession and Career

On talking about his career, Vincent Martella acted in the film entitled Role Models starring Paul Rudd and Seann William Scott.

He then appeared in the film Bait Shop in the lead role along with Billy Ray Cyrus and Bill Engvall.

Not only this, but he also appeared in the show Everybody Hates Chris as Greg and in the show Ned’s Declassified School Survival Guide as Scoop.

Moreover, playing the piano and singing, he released his first album entitled Time Flies By.

Furthermore, Martella entered in the voice acting world providing the voice in the Disney XD animated series Phineas and Ferb for Phineas.

He also lent the English voice in the 2010 video games Final Fantasy XIII and Final Fantasy XIII-2 for Hope Estheim, the main character.

In addition, he lent the voice for the animated film Batman: Under The Red Hood for the teenage Jason Todd / Robin.

There his younger brother, Alexander also lent his voice to the child version of Todd.

Additionally, Vincent Martella performed as Patrick in the fourth season of The Walking Dead television series.
