Damian Priest Gets Custom Money In The Bank Briefcase On 9/4 WWE RAW

September 07, 2024
blog

Damian Priest has received a custom Money in the Bank briefcase.

On the September 4 episode WWE RAW, The Judgment Day celebrated their special night at WWE Payback. At the premium live event, Damian Priest and Finn Balor defeated Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn to win the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Championship. Plus, Rhea Ripley beat Raquel Rodriguez to retain the WWE Women’s World Chamoionship.

The group came to the ring on Monday and made it clear that, despite their recent issues, they clearly run WWE now. Jd McDonagh interrupted, and he was carrying something with him. He revealed that it was a custom Money in the Bank briefcase for Priest.

The custom case has “Senor” Money in the Bank on it, and it is colored purple to match The Judgment Day. In the past, stars like Rob Van Dam, Edge, and others have carried custom Money in the Bank cases.

Priest won the Men’s Money in the Bank Ladder match at WWE Money in the Bank on July 1.
