Kubota B7100 HST Compact  Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 0.8L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD or 4×4 MFWD 4WD, differential mechanical expanding shoe brakes, hydrostatic transmission, and 1151 to 1265 pounds weight.
