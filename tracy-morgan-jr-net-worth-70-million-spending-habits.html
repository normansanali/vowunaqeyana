Tracy’s Mega Mansion

Back in 2015, Tracy Morgan forked over $13.9 million for a home that is so impressive that it seems like it is a dream come true. Built in Alpine, New Jersey, the three-floor mansion included 22 different rooms including seven bedrooms, ten full bathrooms, and 2 half baths. Anytime a home has more bathrooms than bedrooms, you know it was built for the truly wealthy.

Of all the things in the world, it turns out that Tracy Morgan has a passion for collecting undersea animals. For that reason, Morgan has a series of massive water tanks both inside and outside his mansion. In fact, Morgan reportedly spent $400,000 to have only one of his many tanks installed. Thanks to owning all of those tanks, in the past Morgan has been able to buy an eel, seven sharks, two stingrays, and an abundance of other fish.

When Tracy Morgan isn’t busy looking at his many tanks, there were a lot of other things he could do in the New Jersey mansion he bought in 2015. For example, there is a “double-height” basketball court with a custom mural of Morgan’s favorite Knicks players on the wall. If Morgan got bored of shooting hoops, he could always walk over to the bowling alley, check out the movie theater, or enjoy the billiards room.

In terms of the design of the mansion Tracy Morgan bought in 2015, it had oversized windows and chandeliers, a double-wide staircase, and a spacious kitchen with an adjoining breakfast room. Outside the mansion, a large pool that sits beside a cabana with a kitchen and bathroom can be found. In short, Morgan’s mansion practically screams wealth, unlike some stars who prefer to outwardly live like they aren’t rich.

Given all of the money that Tracy Morgan spent on a mansion in 2015, it should go without saying that he doesn’t drive around in average vehicles.
