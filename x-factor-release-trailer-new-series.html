The X Factor Release Trailer For New Series

July 18, 2024
blog

The X Factor has unveiled the first preview of the new series, which is set to commence in the next few weeks.

In the YouTube video revealed by the show, which has gone on to create stars such as Little Mix, JLS, One Direction and Olly Murs, presenter Dermot O’Leary talks viewers through what can be expected from the new series, in the form of a witty poem!

From @onedirection to @LittleMix, who will we discover next? ? #XFactor returns to @ITV… https://t.co/YkmIjjJzda

— The X Factor (@TheXFactor) August 14, 2017

The preview has delighted fans of the show, who have been eagerly anticipating a preview of what can be expected. The Judging panel remains unchanged, following the success of last year’s line-up. Judges Simon Cowell, Nicole Scherzinger, Sharon Osbourne and Louis Walsh will make up the panel on the show this year.

In the preview, it refers to the success of some former contestants over the years from the show – from Little Mix winning a BRIT Award to One Direction’s phenomenal worldwide success and more.
